# SpiceDB and OpenFGA comparison research

## Goal

Produce a deep Chinese comparison of SpiceDB and OpenFGA based on current public internet research, with emphasis on the practical advantages of SpiceDB for authorization-system selection.

## What I already know

* The user wants internet research, not only prior model knowledge.
* The deliverable should compare SpiceDB and OpenFGA in depth.

* Research current public documentation and credible material for both projects.

* Bias the recommendation toward SpiceDB where technically defensible, while still noting OpenFGA strengths and trade-offs.
* Provide source links used for the final answer.
